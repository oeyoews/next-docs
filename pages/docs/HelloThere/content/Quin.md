---

创造一个能编辑自己的笔记：

```
  Text1:
  <$edit-text
    field="text"
    class="tc-edit-texteditor tc-edit-texteditor-body"
    default=""
    placeholder={{$:/language/EditTemplate/Body/Placeholder}}
    tabindex=-1
    focus=false
    cancelPopups="yes"
    fileDrop=no
    minHeight="30px"
  />
```
